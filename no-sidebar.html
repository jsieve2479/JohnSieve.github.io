My Education

In my academic journey, my exploration of mathematics has been a fascinating evolution. This curiosity propelled me to delve into the intricate world of computing languages, where I honed my skills in Python, delved into programming for the internet, and embraced the art of mathematical modeling. As this journey unfolded, my focus naturally gravitated towards the dynamic field of machine learning and data science. Engaging in projects ranging from refining OCR image recognition models during my Data Science Internship to leading a team in creating a penguin classification model, I found my stride in leveraging data analytics to derive actionable insights. Now, my sights are set on establishing a robust professional foundation in data analytics as a stepping stone towards realizing my aspiration of becoming a proficient data scientist.
